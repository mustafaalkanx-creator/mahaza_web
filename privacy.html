Privacy Policy

This privacy policy applies to the mahaza app (Application) for mobile devices, created by Mustafa Alkan (Service Provider) as an Ad Supported service. This service is provided "AS IS".

Information Collection and Use

The Application collects certain information automatically when you download and use it, including:

  • Your device's IP address
  • Pages of the Application visited, including date, time, and duration
  • The operating system and device model used
Data Storage Notice: The Application does NOT use a remote database to store user information. Your selected categories, language preferences, and learning progress are stored locally on your device (localhost/local storage). Clearing app data or uninstalling the app will result in the loss of this information.

Personally Identifiable Information

For a better experience and to enable advertising/analytics, we may require you to provide certain information, including but not limited to:

  • Anonymous Device Identifiers (for advertising purposes)
  • Crash log data and Technical diagnostics
  • Device information (model and OS version)

Third-Party Services & Access

Only aggregated, anonymized data is periodically transmitted to external services to help improve the Application. We utilize the following third-party providers:

  • Google Play Services
  • AdMob (Advertising)
  • PostHog (Product Analytics & Event Tracking)

Children's Privacy

The Service Provider does not knowingly collect information from children under 13. If it is discovered that a child under 13 has provided personal information, it will be immediately deleted from any logs/servers. If you are a parent or guardian and aware of such activity, please contact us.

Security

We are committed to safeguarding your information. We provide physical, electronic, and procedural safeguards to protect the information we process and maintain.

Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. You are advised to consult this page regularly for any changes, as continued use is deemed approval of all changes.
